About the Program

The Executive MBA program at IIM Kozhikode is tailored for professionals who aim to gain advanced management skills without pausing their careers. With a hybrid delivery model, flexible timings, and world-class faculty, this program is a gateway to leadership excellence.

Program Highlights

  • Flexible weekend classes
  • Hybrid (Online + On-campus) delivery
  • Specializations in key management domains

Who Should Apply?

  • Professionals with 5+ years of experience
  • Leaders aiming for career progression
  • Entrepreneurs and senior executives

Admission Process

The application process is straightforward and designed to evaluate your potential for leadership. Follow these steps:

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit required documents (resume, educational certificates).
  3. Attend the personal interview (if shortlisted).
  4. Await final selection and enrollment confirmation.

Curriculum

The curriculum blends foundational management principles with specialized electives to ensure holistic learning:

Core Courses

  • Leadership and Strategy
  • Marketing Management
  • Operations and Supply Chain

Electives

  • Data Analytics
  • Finance and Investments
  • Human Resource Management

Capstone Project

Work on a real-world business challenge to apply your learnings in a practical setting.
